El mar se estremeció primero

Final painting


Illustrated for: Personal project / 3° Congreso Colombiano de Paleontología

This illustration was the winner of the PaleoArt Contest at the 3° Congreso Colombiano de Paleontología in 2025.

It depicts an individual of the ophthalmosaurid ichthyosaur Platypterygius elsuntuoso Páramo-Fonseca et al., 2023 hunting an ammonoid Nicklesia pulchella d'Orbigny.

Initial sketch, self-made 3d model reference for the teeth, and color and value explorations


This illustration was originally intended to accompany the publication by Paramo et al., 2023, which described this new species of ichthyosaur, but life got in the way.
I resumed work on it during the weeks leading up to the 3rd Colombian Paleontology Congress.

Revised sketch, advanced value exploaration, and underwater turbulence rendering attempts
